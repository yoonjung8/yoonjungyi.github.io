About

I'm a behavioural ecologist interested in the social dynamics, cognition, and conservation of primates and other animal species. My work spans field-based behavioural studies, bioacoustics, and multi-species comparisons, with an emphasis on understanding social systems in gibbons, tarsiers, langurs, salamanders, and other vertebrates.

Research

  • Primate social behaviour and ecology (Javan gibbons, tarsiers, langurs)
  • Anti-predator strategies and escape behaviour in amphibians
  • Pollinator foraging behaviour and biodiversity effects
  • Human-wildlife interactions and conservation implications
